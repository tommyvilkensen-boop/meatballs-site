Overview

Meatballs is built like a small magazine: each entry is a point of view — a dish tied to place, tradition, and care. It is meant to read well and age well.

The app is editorial-first. Cook mode exists, but the lens is cultural context: why a dish exists, where it belongs, and what it means before method.

Food apps have become content factories. We wanted the opposite — a book that happens to be an app.

The story

Why meatballs? Because they are universal and specific at the same time — a shared shape with local rules. A dish that travels, adapts, and still remains recognisable. That tension makes it a perfect editorial subject.

Meatballs is an independent project based in Stockholm, built as a long-term reference library rather than a trend-driven feed.

Privacy

Meatballs respects user privacy by design. The app does not require accounts or sign-in. No personal data is collected for advertising or behavioural profiling.

Saved items (such as bookmarks) may be stored on your device and may sync via iCloud to keep your saved items consistent across your own devices. Network requests, when needed, use HTTPS.

Questions regarding privacy: privacy@meatballs.app

Support
