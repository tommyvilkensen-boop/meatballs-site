Frequently Asked Questions

How do I restore my purchase on a new device?

Open the App Store, tap your profile icon, then "Purchased." Find Meatballs and download it again. Your purchase is tied to your Apple ID, so you won't be charged twice.

Does Meatballs work offline?

Yes! Once downloaded, all recipes are stored on your device. No internet connection required to browse or cook.

How do I use Cook Mode?

Open any recipe and tap "Cook" below the title. This keeps your screen awake and shows the current step on your lock screen via Live Activity.

Will there be more recipes?

We're exploring new additions, but curation takes time. Each recipe requires research, testing, and documentation. Quality over quantity, always. When we add new recipes, they'll appear automatically in the app.

Is there an Android version?

Not at the moment. Meatballs is currently iOS only.
