Meatballs

Not a recipe app.

Meatballs is an editorial iOS app exploring one of the world’s most familiar dishes — not as recipes first, but as stories of place, tradition, and care.

What it is App facts Privacy Support & contact

What is Meatballs?

Meatballs is a reference library and cultural archive built around a single dish, seen through countless regional interpretations. From home kitchens to street stalls, from everyday meals to festive tables, Meatballs presents food as lived experience rather than instruction alone.

Each entry combines context, origin, and method — allowing readers to understand why a dish exists before deciding how to cook it. The app is designed to be read like a magazine and explored like an atlas.

Privacy policy

Meatballs respects user privacy by design.

The app does not require accounts or sign-in. No personal data is collected, and the app does not use analytics, ads, or third-party tracking.

Saved items are stored locally and may sync via the user’s own iCloud account to keep bookmarks consistent across the user’s devices.

No data is sold, shared, or processed for advertising purposes.
