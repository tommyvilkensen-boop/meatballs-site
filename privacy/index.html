Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2026

Meatballs is designed with your privacy in mind. We believe good apps don't need your data to be useful.

What we collect

Nothing. Meatballs does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. The app works entirely offline once downloaded.

No accounts

There are no user accounts, no sign-ups, no passwords. You download the app and use it. That's it.

No tracking

We do not use any analytics, tracking pixels, or third-party services that monitor your behavior. We have no idea how you use the app, and we like it that way.

No ads

There are no advertisements in Meatballs, which means no ad networks collecting your data.

Local storage

Your bookmarked recipes are stored locally on your device using iOS's built-in storage. This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to us.

App Store

When you purchase Meatballs, your transaction is handled entirely by Apple through the App Store. We do not receive any of your payment information. Apple's privacy policy applies to that transaction.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, contact us at hello@meatballs.app
